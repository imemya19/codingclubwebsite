MyPolly Botathon
Posted by Coding Club

Have you ever wondered about creating your own Siri or Google Allo? Have you ever thought of the intricacies or the inner working of the brain of a chatbot, or are you just interested in development using Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence?
Well then you’re at the right place, we are organizing a “Botathon”, a Hackathon for creating chatbots sponsored by MyPolly where you’ve to create your own chatbots and test it out in the real world. The Botathon will be of 24 Hours and absolutely zero coding experience is required! also, you can participate alone or form a team at the Botathon venue.
